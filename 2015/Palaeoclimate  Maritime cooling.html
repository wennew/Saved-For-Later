Palaeoclimate: Maritime cooling

Nature Geoscience - Issue - nature.com science feeds by Alicia Newton, 2015-03-31

The Earth's climate has gradually cooled over the past five million years. This cooling initially resulted in a glaciated Northern Hemisphere with moderate glacial–interglacial cycles, and ultimately in the extreme, 100,000-year-long climatic oscillations that have characterized the past million years.
