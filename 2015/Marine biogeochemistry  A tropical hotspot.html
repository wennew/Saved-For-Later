Marine biogeochemistry: A tropical hotspot

Nature Geoscience - Issue - nature.com science feeds by Imke Grefe, 2015-07-01

Nature Geoscience 8, 504 (2015). doi:10.1038/ngeo2473

Author: Imke Grefe

The ocean is an important source of the potent greenhouse gas N2O. Measurements in the tropical South Pacific have revealed a massive efflux of N2O from the coastal upwelling zone.
