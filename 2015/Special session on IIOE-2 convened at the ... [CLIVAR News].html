Special session on IIOE-2 convened at the 26th IUGG General Assembly in Prague

CLIVAR News by Lei Han, 2015-01-23

Abstracts are welcomed to submitted to the special session on the 50th Anniversary of the Indian Ocean Expedition(IIOE-2) that will be convened during the  IAPSO symposium at the IUGG General Assembly in Prague during 26-30 June 2015 (http://www.iugg2015prague.com). The details are available at http://www.iugg2015prague.com/iapso-symposia.htm.
