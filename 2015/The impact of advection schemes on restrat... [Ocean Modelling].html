The impact of advection schemes on restratifiction due to lateral shear and baroclinic instabilities

Ocean Modelling

Highlights

•

We compare advection schemes to investigate their impact on restratification.

•

The schemes are assessed by numerical mixing and dissipation analysis tools.

•

The numerically induced change of background potential energy is calculated.

•

High-accuracy schemes perform best and are recommended for reference simulations.
