Biological oceanography: The CO2 switch in diatoms

Nature Climate Change - Issue - nature.com science feed by Jodi N. Young, 2015-07-24

Diatoms are important primary producers in the ocean, however their response to rising CO 2 is uncertain. Now research shows how diatoms regulate their metabolism in response to changing CO 2 .

Nature Climate Change 5 722 doi: 10.1038/nclimate2691
