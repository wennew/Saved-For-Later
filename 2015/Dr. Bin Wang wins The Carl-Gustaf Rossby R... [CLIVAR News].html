Dr. Bin Wang wins The Carl-Gustaf Rossby Research Medal 2015

CLIVAR News by LinaKang, 2015-01-25

At the 95th Annual Meeting of the American Meteorological Society, the Carl-Gustaf Rossby Research Medal in 2015 was awarded to Dr. Bin Wang, Professor at the Department of Atmospheric Sciences at University of Hawaii, Overseas Dean of the School of Atmospheric Science of NUIST, “for creative insights leading to important advances in the understanding of tropical and monsoonal processes and their predictability”.
