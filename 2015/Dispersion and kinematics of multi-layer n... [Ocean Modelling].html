Dispersion and kinematics of multi-layer non-hydrostatic models

Ocean Modelling

Highlights

•

Conversion of multi-layer framework into Boussinesq form.

•

Function of dispersion effects in tapering flow profile.

•

Analytical solution of dispersion relation for arbitrary number of layers.

•

Convergence of wave dispersion and kinematics as well as their inter-relationship from shallow to extreme deep water.

•

Implementation criteria for numerical applications oriented to both wave celerity and velocity profile.
