Robert Ferraro and Duane E. Waliser

Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of Technology, Pasadena, California;

Peter Gleckler and Karl E. Taylor

Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory, Livermore, California;

Veronika Eyring

Deutsches Zentrum für Luft- und Raumfahrt (DLR), Institut für Physik der Atmosphäre, Oberpfaffenhofen, Germany

This article summarizes the recommendations resulting from a meeting of experts in satellite data products and global climate modeling to plan for the evolution of the obs4MIPs project in support of CMIP6. The meeting took place in Washington DC on April 29 - May 1, 2014.
